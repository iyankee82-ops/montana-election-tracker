2026 Proposed Ballot Issues
MT Secretary of State ↗
Citizen initiatives need 5% of qualified voters (statute) or 10% (constitutional amendment) statewide. Qualifying issues must still gather sufficient verified signatures.
✓ Approved for Signature Gathering
CI-129 · Ballot Issue #1
Primary Residence Property Tax Cap — 2% Annual Limit
Goal: Protect Montana homeowners from rapidly rising property tax assessments by capping annual valuation increases at 2% for primary residences. Aims to provide tax predictability for owner-occupants.
Constitutional amendment limiting primary residence valuation increases to 2%/year. Resets to fair market value on ownership transfer or improvement. Owner must occupy ≥7 months/year.
✓ Approved for signatures: Jan 17, 2025 · Matthew Monforton
CI-130 · Ballot Issue #2
All Real Property Tax Cap — 2% Annual Limit
Goal: Extend the 2% property tax cap to ALL real property in Montana (commercial, agricultural, industrial, timber). Seeks to reduce tax burden on businesses, farms, and ranches.
Same 2% cap applied to all real property classes. Cap resets on ownership transfer or improvements. No assessment may exceed fair market value.
✓ Approved for signatures: Jun 17, 2025 · Matthew Monforton
CI-131 · Ballot Issue #3
Nonpartisan Judicial Elections — Mandate
Goal: Ensure Montana courts remain free from partisan influence by prohibiting party labels on judicial ballots. Responds to legislative efforts to add partisan labels to judicial races.
Constitutional initiative requiring MT Supreme Court and district court elections to be nonpartisan. Prohibits any party labels on judicial ballots.
✓ Approved for signatures: Sep 26, 2025 · Ted Dick
CI-132 · Ballot Issue #5
Nonpartisan Judicial Elections — Constitutional Requirement
Goal: Companion to CI-131 — constitutionally entrench nonpartisan judicial elections, making it harder for future legislatures to introduce partisan judicial races.
Amends MT Constitution to expressly require nonpartisan judicial elections.
✓ Approved for signatures: Oct 9, 2025 · Monica Paoli & Megan Simpson
CI-133 · Ballot Issue #8
Protect Initiative & Referendum Rights
Goal: Strengthen Montanans' right of direct democracy by preventing the government from using procedural obstacles to block citizen ballot initiatives.
Requires impartial, open, predictable processes for petitions and signature verification. Prohibits rejection for minor/technical issues. Allows signature withdrawal.
✓ Approved for signatures: Mar 5, 2026 · Claudia Clifford & Theresa Kendrick
I-194 · Ballot Issue #10
Limit Corporate & Organizational Political Spending
Goal: Get corporate money out of Montana elections by prohibiting corporations, nonprofits, PACs, etc. from contributing to candidate campaigns, parties, or ballot issues.
Violations forfeit Montana business privileges. Exempts news/editorial, government, individuals, political committees.
✓ Approved for signatures: Mar 10, 2026 · Jeff Mangan
